At LIKAGI, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. This Accessibility Policy outlines our efforts to make LIKAGI usable for everyone.

  1. Accessibility Standards
    1. LIKAGI adheres to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, to ensure that our website is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust.
  2. Assistive Technologies
    1. LIKAGI is designed to be compatible with commonly used assistive technologies, including screen readers, magnifiers, and voice recognition software.
  3. Features for Accessibility
    1. LIKAGI includes features to enhance accessibility, such as:
      1. Alternative text for images.
      2. Keyboard navigation support.
      3. High contrast and adjustable font sizes.
  4. Testing and Evaluation
    1. We regularly conduct accessibility assessments and testing to identify and address any issues that may affect the user experience for individuals with disabilities.
  5. User Feedback
    1. LIKAGI welcomes feedback from users regarding accessibility concerns. If you encounter any difficulties using our website, please contact us at [insert contact information].
  6. Ongoing Improvements

  7. Contact Us
    1. If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns about the accessibility of LIKAGI, please contact us at [insert contact information].
  8. Alternate Formats
    1. LIKAGI is committed to providing information in alternate formats upon request. If you require information in a different format, please contact us, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
